# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 25, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 25, 2026

Genesis 1 Technologies, LLC ("Company", "we", "us", "our") operates ShhhType ("the App"), a voice-to-text desktop application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using ShhhType, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account and License Information:** When you purchase a subscription, we collect your email address and payment information through our payment processor, LemonSqueezy. We do not directly store your payment card details.
- **Email for Communications:** If you opt in, we collect your email address to send you product updates, tips, and promotional communications.
- **Support Requests:** If you contact us at help@shhhtype.com, we collect the information you include in your message.

### 1.2 Information Generated by the App

- **Audio Recordings:** When you use ShhhType, the App captures audio from your microphone for the sole purpose of transcription. Audio is processed in real time and is **not stored permanently**. See Section 2 for details on how audio is handled.
- **Transcription Text:** Transcribed text is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database for your personal history. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to use cloud transcription.
- **Machine Identifier:** A hashed version of your macOS hardware UUID is generated locally to activate and validate your software license. This identifier cannot be used to determine your actual hardware UUID.
- **Application Settings:** Your preferences (hotkey configuration, transcription backend choice, overlay position, rewrite style, dictionary entries) are stored locally on your device.

### 1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

- We do **not** collect analytics or telemetry data.
- We do **not** track your usage patterns, keystrokes, or browsing activity.
- We do **not** access or store the content you dictate beyond what is kept in your local transcription history.
- We do **not** use cookies or similar tracking technologies (the App is a native desktop application).

## 2. How Audio Data Is Handled

ShhhType offers two transcription modes. How your audio is processed depends on which mode you select:

### 2.1 Local Transcription (Default)

Audio is processed entirely on your device using a locally downloaded Whisper AI model with Metal GPU acceleration. **No audio data leaves your device.** The audio buffer is discarded immediately after transcription is complete.

### 2.2 Cloud Transcription (Groq API)

If you enable cloud transcription, your audio is:
1. Captured locally and encoded as a 16-bit WAV file;
2. Transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection directly from your device to Groq's API servers;
3. Processed by Groq for transcription and then discarded — Groq does not retain your audio data for model training per their API terms.

**We do not receive, intercept, or store your audio at any point.** The audio is sent directly from your device to Groq. We have no servers that process or relay your audio data.

For details on how Groq handles data, please refer to [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/).

### 2.3 AI Rewrite Feature

If you use the AI rewrite feature, your transcribed text (not audio) is sent to Groq's API for processing by a large language model. The same data handling principles apply — the text is sent directly from your device to Groq and is not retained by Groq for training purposes.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Local Storage

The majority of your data is stored locally on your macOS device at:
```
~/Library/Application Support/com.g1tech.shhhtype/
```

This includes your settings, transcription history, dictionary, and downloaded AI models. This data is protected by your macOS user account permissions and any disk encryption you have enabled (e.g., FileVault).

### 5.2 Security Measures

- All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
- Your machine identifier is a one-way hash that cannot be reversed to obtain your hardware UUID.
- We do not operate servers that store your personal data (except email addresses for communications).
- Audio data is held in memory only during active transcription and is discarded immediately after processing.

### 5.3 Data We Store

The only personal data we store on our systems is:
- Your email address (for license management and opted-in communications)
- Your hashed machine identifier (for license validation)
- Subscription and payment records (maintained by LemonSqueezy)
